Things To Check Out

  • DiscGolfShuffle Mobile App - A mobile app for IOS and Android written in React Native. Designed for casual play in groups, to mix up a normal round with some fun twists.
  • Analysis of Vehicular Accidents in New York City - A data science project, where I worked in a small group to analyze the traffic collisions in New York City before and after the COVID-19 pandemic. We assumed that because of the pandemic, there would be less accidents during this time, as there would be less people on the roads. This report has all of our findings, including graphs, plots, and our development strategies.
  • White Elephant! (Demo here) - A small Web Application I designed for our family White Elephant gift exchange every Christmas. We have some special rules where we roll dice for special rules we have to follow. I turned this into a Web App with a lobby system.
  • Simple Bash Emulator (Demo here) - A fun exercise in string manipulation and data types. I tried to emulate a simple bash terminal, with a handful of commands. I had to design my own data structures to represent files on the "system", and find matching commands from user input. This was great practice at making command line interfaces.
  • JS Canvas Clock (Demo here) - A quick analog clock that runs as a standalone app using Electron. I created this to learn electron, and updated it for demonstration purposes. The demo runs in the browser, with no downloads or installs.
